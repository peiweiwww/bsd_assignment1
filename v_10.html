// TRANSMISSION_PROTOCOL
STEP_01
COMPOSE

Input
Transmission

Author your message through any standard interface. Attachments, recipients, subject headers — all standard fields are supported. The queue is activated on send, not on compose.

STEP_02
ENCRYPTED HOLD

Queue
Activation

Your transmission enters the encrypted hold. AES-256-GCM. Zero-knowledge. Even our infrastructure cannot read the contents. A countdown begins: 86,400 seconds. The message may be recalled at any point before zero.

STEP_03
DELIVERY

Target
Reached

At T+86400, your message is released from the queue and transmitted to its recipient. Unchanged. On time. A delivery receipt is issued to the sender. The hold period cannot be shortened. This is by design.
