Phase 01
Exploration
v1 – v14  ·  14 iterations
Testing what Slowmail could feel like

No constraints, no convergence. Each iteration treats Slowmail as a completely different kind of product — borrowing the visual language of newspapers, record labels, government services, dating apps, and more. The goal was to find which tones felt true to the concept of intentional slowness.

Phase 02
Narrowing Down
v15 – v19  ·  5 iterations
Finding the right register

After fourteen wildly different directions, a pattern emerged — the concept of intentional slowness resonated most with lighter, warmer visual tones. These five versions test that instinct without committing to it: each one restrained, each one considered, each one asking whether calm could be the product's true register.

v15
Japanese Minimalism
Washi paper, Ma concept, gold accents
v16
Gen Z / Playful SaaS
Pastel blobs, bouncy buttons, sticker row
v17
Luxury Fashion House
Gold rules, envelope art, invitation form
v18
Modern SaaS
Clean blue, browser mockup, pricing cards
v19
Wellness / Calm
Sage green, breathing animation, journal cards
Phase 03
Refinement
v20 – v25  ·  6 iterations
Polishing the chosen direction

From these five directions, v19 emerged as the strongest — sage green, warm typography, and a wellness tone that felt true to what Slowmail actually is. Six consecutive iterations refine the architecture, content hierarchy, social proof, product mockup, typography, and narrative flow — converging toward a single, launch-ready design.
