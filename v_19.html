The science of slower

Why 24 hours changes
everything

The pressure of instant communication keeps your nervous system in a constant low-level state of alert. A built-in pause changes that — for you and for everyone you write to.

🧠

Fewer reactive messages

When emotion and send are separated by a night's sleep, the messages that go out are the ones you actually mean — not the ones written in the heat of the moment.

💛

No pressure to reply instantly

When your outgoing messages take a day, you naturally stop expecting instant replies. The whole conversation slows down. Everyone breathes easier.

🌙

Better sleep, less rumination

Sent something you're anxious about? The 24-hour window means you can recall it before morning. Sleep arrives a little easier when email regret isn't permanent.
