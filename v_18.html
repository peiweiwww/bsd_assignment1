Features

Everything you need to
email with intention.

Slowmail is a full-featured email client with one deliberate constraint built in. The 24-hour hold isn't a bug. It's the product.

⏳

24-Hour Send Queue

Every email you send is held for exactly 24 hours. Review it, recall it, or let it send automatically — the choice is yours up until delivery.

Core feature
↩️

Pre-Delivery Recall

Changed your mind? Recall any queued message before it delivers. Available for the full 24-hour hold window, with one click.

🔔

Delivery Notifications

Get notified the moment your message lands. Know exactly when it was read, and how long they took to reply.

📋

Draft Intelligence

Our smart drafting tools surface previous messages to the same contact, keeping context front of mind as you write.

🔒

End-to-End Encryption

Your queued emails are encrypted at rest and in transit. Even our servers can't read what you're sending while it waits.

⚡

Instant Mode

Need to send something now? Toggle Instant Mode for true emergencies. It's opt-in, not the default — and that's intentional.

How it works

Three steps.
One day of patience.

1

Write your email as normal

Compose, attach files, add recipients — Slowmail works exactly like any email client. There's nothing new to learn. Hit send when you're ready.

2

Your message enters the 24-hour hold

Instead of sending immediately, Slowmail queues your email for exactly 24 hours. You'll see it in your Pending folder, with a countdown timer. You can read it, edit it, or recall it at any time before delivery.

3

It delivers — automatically

After 24 hours, your email is sent without any further action from you. You'll get a delivery confirmation, and the message moves to Sent. No surprises, no manual steps.
