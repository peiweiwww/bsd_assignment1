Common questions

Things people
wonder about

Honest answers to the questions we hear most.

What if I need to send something urgent?
Slowmail is for thoughtful correspondence, not time-sensitive alerts. For urgent messages — meeting confirmations, emergencies, quick logistical notes — we recommend keeping a regular email account alongside Slowmail. The 24-hour hold isn't about being slow for slowness's sake; it's about giving your considered words the space they deserve.
Can I cancel an email after I've sent it?
Yes — that's one of the core features. Any email can be recalled at any point during its 24-hour hold. Just open your Outbox, find the message, and hit Recall. It disappears quietly. No notification is sent, no awkward corrections needed. It's as if you never sent it.
Does Slowmail replace my current email, or work alongside it?
Slowmail is a standalone email service with its own address (yourname@slowmail.app). Most users keep both — using Slowmail for personal and professional relationships where care and intention matter, and their existing email for admin and quick logistics. Over time, many find themselves migrating more and more to Slowmail.
Is my email private? Who can read what I send?
Only you and your recipient. We don't scan, train on, or monetise your email content. All messages are encrypted in transit and at rest. We're a small company that makes money through subscriptions, not data. The 24-hour hold happens on your own private outbox — no human eyes see it.
