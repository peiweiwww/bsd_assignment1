Three Principles

I

Compose

Write with the same freedom you have always known. There is no constraint on the act of composition.

Your letter rests with us for one full day. Sealed. Considered. Untouched. This is not a delay. It is a grace.

Tomorrow, without ceremony, your words find their reader. Exactly as written. Entirely as intended.
