About This Album

24 Hours is the debut album from ambient electronic project Slowmail — a meditation on modern communication stripped of its urgency. Each track corresponds to a single hour of delay, building from the anxious energy of composition through the long, hollow stretch of waiting, arriving finally at the quiet relief of delivery.

The album was written entirely during overnight sessions — typed, saved, and left unlistened until the following morning. Nothing was mixed the same night it was recorded. The 24-hour rule applies to every layer.
