✨ How it works

Three steps.
One whole day of patience.

It's really not that complicated. In fact, that's kind of the whole point.

✍️

You write it

Compose your email the same way you always do. Take your time — the 24 hours don't start until you hit send.

💤

We hold it

Your email chills with us for exactly one day. You can read it, edit it, or pull it back. No pressure.

📬

It arrives

Tomorrow, right on time, your message lands in their inbox. Exactly as you wrote it. No surprises.
